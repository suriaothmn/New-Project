About Me

Hi, I’m Ely Suria binti Othman! Born on March 8, 2004, I’m a proud Malaysian who calls the serene town of Tanjong Karang, Selangor, my home. At 160cm, I embrace life with a mix of quiet determination and creative passion.

I have a love for photography—capturing the essence of moments and turning them into stories. Fluent in Malay and English, I navigate the world with curiosity, always open to learning something new.

One of my favorite quotes, “A lot can happen in a year,” reminds me to stay hopeful and embrace change. Despite being a bit shy and reserved, I cherish meaningful conversations, especially when someone takes the first step to connect.

Career Aspirations:
